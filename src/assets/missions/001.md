# Mission // #001

## Arrival

*“We sleep safely at night because rough men stand ready to visit violence on those who would harm us”*  


# Primary Objectives

- Board hostile carrier.
- Disable shipboard propulsion and navigation systems.
- Return to the UNS William Shepherd.

# Secondary Objectives

- Eliminate marked VIPs.
- Disable hostile carrier ship without destroying it.
- Assist friendly operations.

# Stakes

- Operational capacity of the Intervention Fleet
- Information about the size and strength of hostile forces.

---

> The Huygens System has been overtaken by rogue tyrants. In response, Union DOJ/HR initiated the Sierra Madre 21st Critical Assistance Mission to liberate the people of Huygens. They called for volunteers to assist in their mission to spread Utopia. You all answered the call. Union Navy answered the call, committing the 32nd Fleet. After a two year long journey, twenty in objective time, we're about to finally arrive. Heavy resistance is expected. Once the conflict has started, the fleet legion will mark critical targets for boarding action. Disable the target by any means necessary. Mount up pilots, give'em hell.
